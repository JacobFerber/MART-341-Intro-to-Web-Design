Oct 3rd, 2001 - Now

  • I grew up in Edgewood, Washington and graduated from Puyallup Highschool in 2020.
  • My extra cirriculars in Highschool were; being part of the Drumline as a snare drummer, throwing the shotput, diskus, and javelin for the Track team and and eventually becoming the president of the Robotics Club. I was also in the Tacoma Youth Chorus outside of school.
  • In College I have stayed busy by joining a fraternity, adventuring into Montana backcountry, snowboarding, and shooting a few of my own movies.
  • For Sigma Alpha Epsilon, I became the treasurer but for a time I was both house manager and treasurer. This experience has taught me a lot of leadership skills. However, I still struggle with time management skills.
  • This is a natural fault for me because I tend to overcommit myself. Especially with the difficulty of classes increasing I find less and less time for myself and my responsibilities.
  • I do my best applying all of myself to everything I do.
  • On top of all that, my wonderful girlfriend and dog are there to support me every step of the way! Thank you Sara and Billy :)
