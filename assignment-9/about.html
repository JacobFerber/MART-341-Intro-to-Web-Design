About




Why did I enjoy this meal?

My Aunty Susie introduced this dish to my family when we visited them in Port Townsend. Since then, whenever our Mom asked my sister and I about what meals to reccomend for the week. We always replied "Oven Baked Chicken"!!


What advice do I have for beginners?

Play with the level of spice to find what you like. Our family likes it a little hotter but that doesn't mean its the best for you. This meal tastes good with or without the heat!
